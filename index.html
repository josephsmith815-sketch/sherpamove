Common Questions

Everything you need to know

Still have questions? We've got answers.

Is SherpaMove really free to use?

Yes, completely free. We're paid by the moving companies when they receive a lead from us. You never pay anything — not even a hidden fee.

How many quotes will I receive?

Up to 5 verified moving companies will contact you with personalized quotes. You can compare them and choose the best fit — or decline all of them. Zero obligation.

How quickly will movers contact me?

Most movers contact you within 1–2 hours of receiving your details. All movers in our network are required to respond within 24 hours.

Are the moving companies vetted?

Every moving company in our network is verified for licensing, insurance, and operating history. We also monitor customer ratings and remove movers with consistently poor reviews.

Will I get spam calls after submitting?

Your contact details are only shared with the matched movers — never sold to third-party lists. You'll only hear from moving companies relevant to your move.

Can I use SherpaMove for commercial moves?

Absolutely. We have movers who specialize in office and commercial relocations. Just select "Office / Commercial" when filling out your move details.
